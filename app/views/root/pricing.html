Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 30-day free trial work?

To explore the functionality of Meetupedia Basic or Pro plans simply sign-up and test at no cost for 30 days. We'll let you know when your trial is almost over so you can choose between Basic, Pro and Free accounts.

Can I upgrade my plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade your account from your Meetupedia dashboard any time you want.

What subscription options do I have for Meetupedia Basic and Meetupedia Pro?

Meetupedia offers both monthly and annual subscriptions. For monthly subscriptions, there are no term-length commitments. For yearly subscriptions there discounts.

What methods of payment do you accept?

We currently accept payments online by Visa and Mastercard.

What support do you provide?

All Meetupedia customers can utilize the extensive help desk article archive and community forums at help.meetupedia.com. Trouble tickets from Enterprise accounts, and Pro customers with Meetupedia, receive prioritized responses.

Have more questions before you sign up?

To learn more about Meetupedia's technical functionality, please review the articles at the Help Desk. Still can't find your answer? Submit a ticket and we will get back to you soon.
